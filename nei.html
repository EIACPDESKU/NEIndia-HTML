Flora and Fauna

Arunachal Pradesh

Flora:

  • Orchids (Various species): Known as the "Orchid Paradise of India," Arunachal Pradesh boasts over 500 species of orchids, many of which are rare and endemic.
  • Rhododendrons (Rhododendron spp.): The state's temperate forests are home to numerous species of rhododendrons.
  • Medicinal Plants (Various species): Arunachal Pradesh is a treasure trove of medicinal plants used in traditional healing systems.
  • Bamboo (Various species): Bamboo is abundant in Arunachal Pradesh and has numerous practical and cultural uses.
  • Coniferous Forests (Pinus spp., Abies spp., Picea spp.): Found in the higher reaches of the state, dominated by pine, fir, and spruce trees.

Fauna:

  • Red Panda (Ailurus fulgens): One of the few places in India where this elusive mammal is found.
  • Snow Leopard (Panthera uncia): Found in the rugged Himalayan terrain of Arunachal Pradesh.
  • Takin (Budorcas taxicolor): Known as "gnoo-sing" in local dialects, found in the Mishmi Hills and other regions.
  • Himalayan Black Bear (Ursus thibetanus): Inhabits the dense forests of the state.
  • Hornbills (Buceros bicornis and Rhyticeros undulatus): Includes species like the great hornbill and the wreathed hornbill.
  • Golden Cat (Catopuma temminckii): A rarely seen and elusive wildlife highlight.

Nagaland

Flora:

  • Alder (Alnus nepalensis): The state tree of Nagaland.
  • Rhododendron (Rhododendron arboreum): The state flower of Nagaland.
  • Blue Vanda (Vanda coerulea): A unique orchid species exclusive to the region.
  • Medicinal Plants (Various species): Over 500 species with potential therapeutic properties.

Fauna:

  • Mithun (Bos frontalis): The semi-domesticated gaur and state animal of Nagaland.
  • Blyth's Tragopan (Tragopan blythii): The state bird of Nagaland.
  • Clouded Leopard (Neofelis nebulosa): A unique species found in Nagaland.
  • Hoolock Gibbons (Hoolock hoolock): A rare species inhabiting the state’s forests.
  • Golden Langur (Trachypithecus geei): Another unique primate species found in the region.
  • Himalayan Black Bear (Ursus thibetanus): Also found in Nagaland.
  • Naga Tree Frog (Polypedates nagaensis): A unique amphibian species of the region.
  • Viper Family Snake (Various species, Viperidae): Found in Nagaland's biodiversity.
